About Me

Having experience in multiple engineering and construction projects worth billions of dollars, I had to work with gigabites of big data, generated by these processes. Trying to find the best and most efficient way of processing of such data I discovered the world of Data Science and Big Data, which fascinates me a lot.


My another passion is Bioinformatics and Genomic Science. I consider that they shape the future of humanity and do my best to become the part of this future.


I passed multiple courses, including IBM and John Hopkins University and dived into the sea of Big Data, Data Science and Bioinformatics.
